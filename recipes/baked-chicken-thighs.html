Easy Baked Chicken Thighs

Description

Low on time? Give this quick & delicious chicken thigh dinner recipe a try.
Done in under 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken thighs
  • 4 Teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 Teaspoons onion flakes

Steps

  1. Step 1
    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C)
  2. Step 2
    Place chicken thighs in a baking dish;season both sliders with garlic powder and onion flakes.
  3. Step 3
    Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes.
    An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
